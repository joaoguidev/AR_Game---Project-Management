The game idea is to develop a tabletop AR single-player strategy game where the match will consist of a confrontation between the player and the opponent controlled by the computer. The player will be awarded points throughout the match and a scoreboard will be there to each level in order to keep track of the player's performance. The idea is also to try to add elements such as wind (which is easy to do), rain (which will change the movement speed depending on the terrain type the unity is moving) and fluid simulation (depending on how hard it will hit performance).
