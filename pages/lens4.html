Mechanics: The player will be able to move and explore confined dungeons like Diablo but in AR. The player will be able to control the character and walk around and attack enemies by pointing to the gaze marker. Initially, the game will allow players to only walk and attack. Depending on how fast the project is developed new mechanics will be implemented like inventory, weapons, potions, grenades... Other development of the game is a companion that helps the player and follow you around (Fallout style). See Companion
